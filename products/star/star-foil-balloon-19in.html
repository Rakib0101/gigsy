    Products Warning and Disclaimer
    1. Optimal Transport: Safely transport up to 24 inflated balloons in the back seat of your vehicle.
    2. Temperature Matters: Maintain the aesthetic appeal of your balloons by storing them at room temperature—keep them warm on chilly days and cool on warmer ones.

    5. Responsible Disposal: After your celebration, responsibly dispose of uninflated balloons.
